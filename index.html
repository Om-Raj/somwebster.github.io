Instify

Overview

Overviewmore_vert

Instify is a mini social networking android application, which acts as a companion for college and school students. This app allows the users to get instant notifications about the college/school news and announcements, keep track of what is trending in the campus, share academic notes, keep track of attendance and marks, get timetable related alerts on a daily basis and much more.
